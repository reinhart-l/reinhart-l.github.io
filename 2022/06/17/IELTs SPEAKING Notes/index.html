IELTs, 个人

听不懂时

  • 如果完全不懂
    • sorry?
    • could you say that again pls?
  • 不懂某个词
    • What’s the meaning of XXX?
    • What does XXX mean?
  • 如果大概懂
    • Do you mean XXX ?

词汇和表达

  • earn my own bread

  • big fortune

  • Starry night

  • picturesque 风景如画的 adj.

  • Underpaid = not paid well 酬不抵劳

  • By reading extensively, students can gain/obtain/acquire/expand their knowledge in different fields.

  • aurora 极光

  • the milky way 银河

  • shooting star/meteor 流星

  • intriguing 有趣的

  • tedious 冗长的 乏味的

  • bunk bed 高低床

  • Thrilling plots 刺激情节

  • Thriller / horror movie 恐怖片

  • Recharge one’s batteries/loosen up

  • be immersed in

  • Hustle and bustle 生活的烦嚣

  • crack sb. up 逗笑

  • punch line 梗

  • burst into laughter

  • it hinges on sth.=it depends

  • Lie in/wake up naturally 赖床/自然醒

  • have one-day off 有一天的休息

  • Apply oneself to do sth.

  • be doom to failure / end in failure 失败

发音

Harmonious

mistake I made

经常破句！！！->先说句子主干，一定要说完整的句子出来

  • on the weekdays/At the weekend

Part1

答题逻辑

  • direct response+(reason)+details/examples
  • 不能讲原因的时候 focus on details(relevant experience->when../every time…/the moment…)
  • 当问题用虚拟语气提问，要用过去式和过去将来时回答u

答题技巧

  • 要注意不要犯基础语法错误

  • 具像化:eat food => eat sth./delicacies/snacks

  • 如果不太熟悉问题：I’m not a big fan with…/it’s not my cup of tea, but I can try…

  • 尽量多用话题词

  • 先说句子主干

GA

  • 简单句复合句结合

  • 时态的准确运用

  • 从句

  • 比较级&最高级

  • 被动语态

使用无意义连接词

  • Well, …

  • Actually, …

  • You know, (众所周知的内容)…

  • In fact, …

  • To be honest/frank, …

  • Honestly/ Frankly, …

  • What I mean is …

  • I suppose …

有意义的连接词

时间、地点、方式、目的状语提前，可以争取思考时间

  • 时间

    • In 2020,
    • Last summer vacation,
    • In my childhood,
    • Currently,
    • At the moment,
    • In the recent years,

  • 地点

    • At school,
    • At home,
    • On the campus, ..
    • In China,
    • In some foreign countries, …

  • 目的

    • In order to do …,

  • 方式

    • By doing, …
添加信息
  • Also, …
  • Besides,…
  • What’s more,…
  • Morover,…
  • In addition,…
  • Additionally,…
  • On top of that, …
转折/对比/让步
  • but
  • On the other hand,…
  • On the contrary, …
  • while…
  • although…
  • However, …
举例
  • For instance, …
  • For example, …
  • such as …,
  • like…

拓展details的方式

1.当所问的问题完全没有涉及过时

  • 用if

  • 用虚拟语气

    • If I were…, I would do…
    • If i were a teacher, i wouldn’t care about the students’ academic performance.
    • If i were a mayor, i wouldn’t know how to give a presentation …

  • eg

    • Have you ever tried any extreme sports?

      No, I haven’t, because it’s very terrifying. When i was young, my uncle, who was a skateboarder, broke his legs in a competition. (At that moment, I swore that i would never tried this kind of sports. )

2.分类讨论，对比

  • ==it depends（on sth）. / Well, it hinges on sth.==

  • I usually enjoy …, but I also tend to/I also am likely to do …. from time to time.

    Doing sth. is a good choice for me, ….. But,/ Yet,/ However, I may do …every once in a while.

eg

1.What do you do to relax?
Regularly- do some workouts to take a break You know, sitting in front of the computer for a whole day is pretty tiring.
However, during a longer holiday-> travel/ historical sites.

2.Are you into car journey?

It depends.- As a driver/, going on a road trip/ concentrate on the road/ traffic/ ..,which makes me nervous …
If i am a passenger/ chat freely / that’s okay for me

更多对比法

  • 时空对比

    • (used to do/ but currently)

  • 相反意见(P3常用)

Is your hometown a nice place for tourists to visit?

I would say yes, because there are a number of picturesque tourist attractions, such as …. .（Sun Yatsan Memorial Hall） Although it’s getting more and more crowded, the visitors can still travel to their destinations efficiently by public transport.

      • with excitement

开头

How you knew this person(+first impression)
  • Since i was in high school, I have known him for donkey’s years. On the first day of school, the moment i saw him, i was impressed by his broad smile and sparkling / cheerful eyes. Gradually, i found he was really an outgoing person.
如何使appearance和personality 相关

  • lanky(tall & thin) -basketballer / basketball player

  • Muscular - athlete

  • Overweight - Nowadays, a great number of people are crazy about fast food, so some of them have become overweight.

  • petite 娇小的

    • 正向关联： she is a shy and petite girl.
    • 反向关联： Although she is a little bit petite, she is one of the most courageous girls I’ve ever met.
Personality PlanA
1 personality+3 例子

  • Social/ gregarious / outgoing

    1. Greet others with a broad smile / every time she meets someone on campus

    2. Take part in some volunteer activities/ be willing to give a hand to the people in need

    3. In class/ raise her hand / first one / to answer the teachers’ questions

  • Every time she meets her acquaintances on campus, she always greets them with a broad smile. During the summer vacation, she definitely takes part in some volunteer activities and she is willing to give a hand to the people in need. In class, she is always the first one to raise her hand to answer the teachers’ questions.

  • Industrious/ diligent

    • In order to achieve her goal in a competition, she applies herself to program and analyze the data and even sleeps over in the lab.
    • During the /Every summer holiday, she gives up her leisure time in order to gain some knowledge about her major in advance.
    • Moreover, when she has done some experiments with bad results, she still doesn’t give up and try it again and again.

  • Sweet-tempered

    • Every time we have a group discussion about our assignment at school, sometimes we argue with each other, but she is always the most patient listener.Somehow, when people misunderstand her, instead of losing her temper to fight with the gossipers, she doesn’t care about it at all. During the summer vacation, she always invites us to have a home-made dinner together, which makes us feel warm.
